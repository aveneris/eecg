### Biography

Ashvin Goel is a Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Toronto. His research interests lie broadly in systems software, including cloud computing, storage systems, high-performance software systems, and reliable systems. He has published in prestigious systems conferences including SOSP, OSDI, ASPLOS, FAST and Eurosys. He received his PhD in computer science and engineering from Oregon Graduate Institute (OGI) in Portland and his masters from UCLA.

### Research Interests

Operating systems, with a focus on reliability and security
